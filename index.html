DTLP Program

This leadership program is a two-year program with four rotational job assignments focused on GE’s top digital initiatives. Program members lead by doing—gaining real-world experience by working on important and challenging projects alongside some of the brightest minds in the world.

As a pipeline for future leaders of GE’s world-class global digital technology organization, our Digital Technology Leadership Program has partnered with academic and industry training providers to deliver continued education in technology, management and leadership. This training includes in-person classes inside and outside of the U.S., and other virtual electives.

Each assignment and training experience is global. If you are looking to make a measurable impact on the way GE does business, gain exposure to senior level leaders, and expand your global mindset and network by working and training with colleagues from around the world—your wait is over. GE’s Digital Technology Leadership Program is looking for qualified candidates interested in building the foundation for a rewarding career.

Internships are also available through an 8-12 week experience where you have the opportunity to make your mark at GE. A DTLP internship is a great way to set yourself apart from your peers and gain early-access to interviews for the full-time leadership program.

About GE

General Electric (GE) is an American multinational conglomerate corporation incorporated in New York.
